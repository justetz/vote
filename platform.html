My Platform


Have a question? Want to share an idea? Just want to say hi? Awesome!
Shoot me an email here.

As a senator, I pledge to embody the following creed throughout my term:

I will sit down with my constituents, and I will stand up for my constituents.

Be it trivial or earth-shattering, I will address every student concern brought to my attention with tact and dedication.

I will sit down with you. I want to hear your opinions on issues that affect you. I want to know your take on every situation. I want to know if I am missing anything; no one person can fully understand the entire scope of any project. I want to bring your views to the table.

I will stand up for you. I will bring your ideas to Senate and committee meetings and relay them to project leaders. I will address your concerns with relevant RPI officials to assist in a resolution.

That promise to each and every one of my constituents is already in effect. As always, you can reach me for any reason.
